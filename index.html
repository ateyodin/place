Departments

OneTechPlace, Inc. provides Information Technology related analysis, planning, programming and development, quality assurance, implementation, organizational consulting, out-sourcing and support services to or for our customers.
Our principles and senior consultants have, on average, 15 years experience in delivering focused, business process and technical solutions on time and within budget.
Our client base includes satisfied customers throughout multiple State and Federal agencies, municipal governments; and the university system as well as the private sector business community.
We are experienced in working with or through Services departments or with individual offices, divisions, and agencies or at an enterprise-wide level.

Providing turn-key or integrated support for all or any part of the development cycle from analysis and planning though delivery, knowledge-transfer, user training and support in the following practice areas:
Technology based programs and systems, business-process mapping and valuation analysis, and customer-service needs and initiatives planning.
New, on going and existing development programs and re-engineering initiatives.
Professional and support services on a short range or long-term basis.
Contractual planning and program staging services designed to justify planned and future information technology expenditures and to integrate those expenditures with your budget cycle.
Utilizing a business and customer oriented approach that is designed to identify needs, establish measures of success within the client's environment and demonstrate our client's return on investment for our services.
This approach results in deliverables that:
Enhance management's decision support systems through the use of business intelligence. Develop and demonstrate measurable success factors. Position our clients to move to the next level of improved program measures, realization of cost savings and enhanced customer service.
Delivering solutions through Information Services departments on a contracted services or program development basis.
Supporting the information technology needs of individual offices, divisions or agencies whose needs cannot be currently met by available in-house information technology services. This level of service can be accomplished while maintaining the Information Systems Development Methodology (ISDM) standards of the client's organization or choice.
Providing, through our mix of employees, consultants and sub-contractors the ability to rapidly expand or contract the level of our services to meet the client's changing needs and resources.

Speciality

SPECIALTY DELIVERABLES

Please note that our firm has significant experience in developing, implementing and training users in web-based documentation and user support systems. These web-based systems have the significant advantage of:

• Little or no client-side application support, relying exclusively on standardized browsers on the desktop or laptop. Any authorized user can access these applications with the correct access levels by internal and external customers - inside or outside of the organization.

• Realization of significant cost and time advantages by the customer. The speed of delivery for revisions and additions is greatly improved, thereby breaking the costly cycle of desktop installation and re-installation throughout the organization or customer base.

• Retention and control of source code on the host side rather than potential exposure at the client desktop level.

• Additional advantages are realized from on-line documentation of legacy and new systems by speeding troubleshooting at the Beta and/or customer support level.

• Speeding the life cycle of new development initiatives or enhancements to existing systems.

• Enhancing the ability to provide quick turnaround for Quality Assurance and regression testing efforts.
